Our Mission

LUMEN Initiative is formed exclusively for charitable, educational, scientific, and literary purposes within the scope of 501(c)(3) organizations with no private inurement. As students ourselves, we focus on providing education to other youths, especially those who are underserved, in the community with hands-on experience as the emphasis.

We aim to foster interests in areas including but not limited to astronomy, physics, Classics, computer science, and mathematics through interactive workshops, experiments, and community events.
